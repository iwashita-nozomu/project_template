# Critical Review

1. Read `agents/skills/critical-review.md`.
1. Check comparison fairness.
1. Separate observed results from interpretation.
1. Look for missing baselines, small sample artifacts, and failure cases hidden by averages.
1. Decide whether the outcome is `report_rewrite_required`, `extra_validation_required`, `rerun_required`, or `approved`.
1. Use `documents/experiment-critical-review.md`.
